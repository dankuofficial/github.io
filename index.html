Welcome to AI Zavrushka

AI Zavrushka is an internal automation tool that helps publish and schedule Pins on Pinterest using the official Pinterest API.

This application is intended for private or business use only. It is not affiliated with or endorsed by Pinterest.

Key Features:

  • Automated Pin creation
  • Scheduling Pins for specific dates and times
  • Integration with business Pinterest accounts
  • Multi-board publishing support

With this app, users can streamline their content publishing workflows and keep their Pinterest boards active and engaging.
